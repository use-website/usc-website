We provide expert fire safety consultancy to help organisations meet their legal duties and manage fire risk effectively.

Our approach is practical and proportionate, focusing on real-world risks, clear guidance, and compliance with current fire safety legislation.

### Our fire safety consultancy services include:
- Fire risk assessments  
- Review of fire safety policies and procedures  
- Advice on means of escape and fire precautions  
- Liaison with enforcing authorities  
- Ongoing fire safety support and guidance  

All fire safety work is carried out by competent professionals with experience across a range of sectors.
